Biography

I am currently working on Modeling climate trends and variability in the Himalaya to understand cryosphere changes at the Institute of Environmental Geosciences (Grenoble, France). My supervisors are Martin Ménégoz and Gerhard Krinner. We are in close collaboration with IPSL in Paris using the LMDZ model.
