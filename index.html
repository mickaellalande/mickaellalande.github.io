Biography

I am currently PhD student at the Institute of Environmental Geosciences (Grenoble, France), working on Modeling climate trends and variability in High Mountain Asia to understand cryosphere changes (supervised by Martin Ménégoz and Gerhard Krinner). We have a strong collaboration with the IPSL, using their ORCHIDEE and LMDZ models.
